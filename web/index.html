Daily Stroke Bites

Quick tips and facts to keep you informed and motivated every day

Did you know? 1 of 6

The brain can form new neural pathways for years after stroke. Every therapy session helps build these connections ‚Äî this is called neuroplasticity.

Recovery tip 2 of 6

The fastest recovery happens in the first 3 months, but improvement can continue for years. Never give up ‚Äî your brain is always capable of change.

Exercise 3 of 6

Even 10 minutes of daily movement can reduce the risk of a second stroke by up to 25%. Start small ‚Äî every step counts toward your recovery.

Mental health 4 of 6

About 1 in 3 stroke survivors experience depression. It's not weakness ‚Äî it's a common part of recovery. Talking to someone can make a real difference.

Sleep 5 of 6

Your brain heals during sleep. Stroke survivors need more rest than before ‚Äî 7-9 hours at night plus short naps are completely normal and beneficial.

FAST 6 of 6

Know the signs: Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time to call emergency. Quick action saves brain cells.
