## Phase 2: Engagement

### Stroke Bites Content

**Requirement:** 100-200 cards minimum for 3-6 months of daily content

| Category | Quantity | Example |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Recovery Facts | 40+ | "The brain can form new neural pathways for years after stroke ‚Äî this is called neuroplasticity." |
| Daily Tips | 40+ | "Try brushing your teeth with your affected hand today for fine motor practice." |
| Motivation | 30+ | "Recovery isn't linear. A hard day doesn't erase your progress." |
| Nutrition | 20+ | "Foods rich in omega-3s like salmon may support brain health." |
| Exercise | 20+ | "Seated exercises can be just as effective as standing ones." |
| Mental Health | 20+ | "It's normal to feel frustrated. Acknowledge the feeling, then let it pass." |

**Content Sources:**
- American Stroke Association publications
- National Stroke Association
- Peer-reviewed stroke recovery research
- Licensed medical content writers
- Survivor interviews (with permission)

**Content Requirements:**
- Medically accurate
- Written at 6th-8th grade reading level
- Positive/encouraging tone
- Under 150 words per card
- Include source citation for facts

## Phase 3: Community

### Seed Content

**Requirement:** 10-20 recovery stories to launch with

| Content Type | Quantity | Source |
|--------------|----------|--------|
| Recovery stories | 10-20 | Interview survivors, stroke associations |
| Sample posts | 20-30 | Written examples to set tone |
| Community guidelines | 1 document | Write internally |

### Recovery Story Template

```
Title: [Inspiring title]
Stroke Date: [When it happened]
Stroke Type: [Type]
Current Status: [X months/years post-stroke]

My Story:
[2-5 paragraphs about their experience]

What Helped Most:
[Key insights]

Advice for Others:
[Encouragement for new survivors]
```

### Community Guidelines

```
1. Be supportive and kind
2. No medical advice ‚Äî share experiences, not diagnoses
3. Respect privacy ‚Äî don't share others' information
4. No spam or self-promotion
5. Report concerning content
6. This is a safe space ‚Äî no judgment
```

### Moderation Rules

| Content Type | Action |
|--------------|--------|
| Medical misinformation | Remove + warning |
| Spam/promotion | Remove + ban |
| Harassment | Remove + ban |
| Self-harm mention | Flag for review + resources |
| Off-topic | Gentle redirect |

## Phase 5: Integrations

### FAST Stroke Signs Content

```
F - FACE: Ask the person to smile. Does one side droop?
A - ARMS: Ask to raise both arms. Does one drift down?
S - SPEECH: Ask to repeat a phrase. Is speech slurred?
T - TIME: Call emergency services immediately if any signs present.

Emergency Number: [Localized - 911 US, 999 UK, etc.]
```
