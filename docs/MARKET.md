# Strokecovery - Market Analysis

## Market Overview

### Stroke Statistics

| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Global stroke incidence | 12.2 million new strokes/year | WHO 2023 |
| US stroke incidence | 795,000 strokes/year | CDC |
| Stroke survivors (US) | 7+ million | American Stroke Association |
| TBI incidence (US) | 2.8 million/year | CDC |
| Average recovery time | 3 months to 2+ years | Medical literature |

### Market Size

| Segment | Value | Growth |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Global stroke rehabilitation market | $5.2B (2023) | 7.8% CAGR |
| Digital health apps market | $350B (2023) | 25% CAGR |
| mHealth apps market | $100B (2023) | 17% CAGR |

### Key Insight

> Stroke is the leading cause of long-term disability. Survivors need ongoing support for months to years — yet most leave the hospital with little more than pamphlets.

## Target Audience

### Primary Segments

| Segment | Size | Characteristics |
|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Stroke Survivors** | 7M+ (US) | Ages 45-75, need daily medication, therapy tracking |
| **TBI Patients** | 2.8M+ (US) | Younger (20-50), similar recovery needs |
| **Caregivers** | 10M+ (US) | Family members, often overwhelmed, need tools |

### User Personas

**Persona 1: Recent Stroke Survivor**
- Age: 62
- Tech comfort: Moderate
- Needs: Medicine reminders, therapy tracking, hope
- Frustrations: Forgets medications, feels isolated
- Quote: "I just want to get back to normal"

**Persona 2: Long-term Survivor**
- Age: 55
- Tech comfort: High
- Needs: Community connection, progress tracking
- Frustrations: Feels forgotten by healthcare system
- Quote: "Nobody tells you recovery takes years"

**Persona 3: Family Caregiver**
- Age: 45
- Tech comfort: High
- Needs: Monitor loved one, coordinate care
- Frustrations: Can't be there 24/7, burnout
- Quote: "I worry constantly when I'm not with them"

## Market Push vs Pull Analysis

### Verdict: Strong Market PULL

This app benefits from organic demand rather than requiring heavy paid advertising.

### Pull Factors (Organic Demand)

| Factor | Explanation |
|--------|-------------|
| **High-intent moment** | Stroke is a crisis. Families actively search for help |
| **Desperate need** | No dominant solution exists for post-discharge support |
| **Natural referral channels** | Doctors, therapists, hospitals can recommend |
| **Strong word-of-mouth** | Patient communities actively share resources |
| **Caregiver research** | Family members Google extensively after diagnosis |

### Distribution Channels (No/Low Cost)

| Channel | Strategy | Cost |
|---------|----------|------|
| **Healthcare providers** | Partner with neurologists, therapists, rehab centers | Free (relationship building) |
| **Stroke associations** | American Stroke Association, local groups | Free (partnership) |
| **Hospital discharge programs** | Include in patient materials | Free (B2B partnership) |
| **Online communities** | Facebook stroke groups, Reddit r/stroke | Free (organic) |
| **SEO/Content marketing** | Blog posts: "stroke recovery tips" | Low (time investment) |
| **App Store Optimization** | Keywords: stroke, TBI, recovery | Free |

### When Paid Ads Make Sense

| Scenario | Channel | Timing |
|----------|---------|--------|
| Scaling beyond organic | Facebook/Instagram | After product-market fit |
| Reaching younger TBI demographic | TikTok, YouTube | Phase 3+ |
| B2B healthcare sales | LinkedIn | Phase 4+ |

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Soft Launch

1. **Closed beta** — 20-50 users from stroke support groups
2. **Gather feedback** — Iterate on core features
3. **Build testimonials** — Video testimonials from early users

### Phase 2: Public Launch

1. **App Store launch** — iOS + Android
2. **Press release** — Healthcare media outlets
3. **Association partnerships** — American Stroke Association feature
4. **Content marketing** — Blog, SEO, social media presence

### Phase 3: Growth

1. **Healthcare partnerships** — Hospital discharge programs
2. **Therapist network** — Referral program for OTs, PTs, SLPs
3. **Paid acquisition** — Retargeting, lookalike audiences
4. **International expansion** — Localize for UK, Australia, India
